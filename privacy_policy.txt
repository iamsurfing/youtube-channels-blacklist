Information We Collect
This extension does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. However, the following data is stored locally on your browser and may be synced across devices if you are signed into Chrome:

Blacklisted Channels: The list of YouTube channels you choose to blacklist is stored in Chrome's synced storage. This data is not shared with third parties and is only used to filter content on YouTube based on your preferences.
Permissions
To function properly, the YouTube Channel Blacklist extension requires certain permissions:

Storage: We use chrome.storage.sync to store your list of blacklisted YouTube channels. This allows the extension to remember your preferences across sessions and devices (if you are signed into Chrome).

ActiveTab: This permission allows the extension to interact with the currently open YouTube tab in your browser. It is required to modify the content of the YouTube page and hide videos from blacklisted channels.

Scripting: We use scripting to inject code into YouTube pages that identifies and hides videos from blacklisted channels.

Host Permissions (YouTube): The extension requires access to YouTube domains (https://www.youtube.com/* and https://m.youtube.com/*) to perform its function. It reads page content to identify video channels and filters content based on your blacklist.

Third-Party Data Sharing
The YouTube Channel Blacklist extension does not share, sell, or distribute any of your personal data or stored preferences with third parties. Your data remains local to your browser and is only used to enhance your browsing experience on YouTube.
